PHISH MAYAN REDESIGN
The hieroglyphs of the ancient Maya is one of the worlds most beautiful forms of writing, however, stone carvings often yielded sloppy lines and hard-to-read glyps. I thought it deserved a makeover. My goal for this project was to design a Mexico themed screen-printed concert poster for the fans the Rock and Roll superstars Phish. I wanted to capture the beauty of the ancient Maya writings, while invoking a feeling of modernity, elegance, and relaxation.
GETTING STARTED

The first step to my process is demographic analysis. From a poll of 2438 Phish Fans or "Phans" I learned that 44.5% of Phish Fans or "Phans" are 25-34 years old, and 27.2% are 35-44 years old.
